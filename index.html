Get to Know Us

How it all started...

Oneida Warehousing, LLC (Oneida) was formed in 2000 by Peter Marshall, Lawrence R Ellis III, and William C. Ellis. Collectively, the members brought over 100 years of warehousing and logistics experience to the organization.

Customer Focus

→

Our process is simple, we spend the time to get to know our customers and understand their unique supply chain requirements. Then, we customize a solution that meets our customers' needs while maximizing efficiency and customer service. We are eager to invest in the most modern equipment, latest technology, and facilities where our customers need us to be.

Employee Satisfaction

We strive to treat our employees well, and have significantly lower turnover than industry standards. We feel the better treated our employees are, the better they will treat our customers, and our customers' customers. We strive to have a postive influence on the communities we operate in.

Rail Capabilities
